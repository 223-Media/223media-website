üé® Design Specifications

Your favicon should use these EXACT brand colors:
  • Background: Gradient from #200c2a (dark purple) to #786c7e (light purple)
  • Border: #f17000 (orange) - 2-3px border
  • Text "223": #f17000 (orange) or #ffffff (white)
  • Shape: Circular design matching your logo
