ALEX CHEN - TECHNOLOGY FUTURIST

Leading voice in emerging technology with 15+ years of experience in AI research, venture capital, and startup ecosystems. From Stanford AI labs to Silicon Valley boardrooms, Alex bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world applications.

Background & Expertise

Alex Chen's journey began at Stanford's AI Research Lab, where they contributed to breakthrough research in neural networks and machine learning. After completing a PhD in Computer Science with a focus on artificial intelligence, Alex transitioned to venture capital, investing in over 50 technology startups.

With a unique perspective spanning academia, industry, and investment, Alex has witnessed firsthand how technologies evolve from research papers to world-changing products. This experience forms the foundation of Tech Forward's insightful analysis.

Today, Alex serves as a technology advisor to Fortune 500 companies, an angel investor in emerging tech startups, and a frequent speaker at major technology conferences worldwide.
